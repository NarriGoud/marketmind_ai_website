MarketMind External Chatbot User Guide

Welcome to the user guide for the MarketMind External Chatbot. This document explains how to use and integrate the MarketMind AI chatbot on your website, what features it supports, and important notes to ensure a smooth experience.

What is the MarketMind External Chatbot?

The MarketMind External Chatbot is a ready-to-use chat interface that can be embedded on external websites to provide users with real-time financial insights, news, and sentiment analysis focused on Indian stock markets.

It offers an interactive and easy-to-use interface for visitors to:

  • Ask questions about the latest market news.
  • Get sentiment insights on Indian stocks.
  • Access detailed explanations to understand market context.

Important Notes

  • Indian Stock Market Focus:
    Currently, the chatbot supports only Indian stock tickers and related financial news. This restriction helps ensure focused, relevant, and accurate insights.

  • Future Expansion Plans:
    MarketMind intends to expand coverage to include global stock markets in regional markets such as the US, Europe, and Asia in upcoming releases.

  • Natural Language Queries:
    Users can ask questions using plain English without needing special commands or codes. The chatbot understands and responds with helpful data and sentiment analysis.

  • No Personal Data Storage:
    The external chatbot does not store user-specific data such as watchlists or preferences on partner websites. It delivers on-demand information without persistent user profiling.

  • Watchlist Management:
    Watchlist creation, management, and alerting are exclusively available on the MarketMind Telegram Bot.
    The external chatbot does not offer watchlist features or notifications.

How to Use the Chatbot on Your Website

You can embed the chatbot by adding a small JavaScript snippet to your webpage. Once embedded, the chatbot will appear as an interactive widget that adapts to different screen sizes, including mobile devices.

Key Usage Points

  • The chatbot automatically adjusts its size based on the device viewport to ensure a user-friendly experience on mobile and desktop browsers.
  • It prevents multiple instances from loading on the same page to avoid overlap or conflicts.
  • All configuration happens dynamically from MarketMind’s backend, so no manual setup is needed after embedding.

Important User Features

  • Natural Language Interaction: Users can ask questions in plain English about companies, stock tickers, or recent news.
  • Real-Time Sentiment Insights: The chatbot provides sentiment classification (positive, neutral, negative) along with explanations.
  • Responsive Design: The chatbot interface adjusts automatically to suit desktop and mobile screens.
  • Consistent Branding: The chatbot uses MarketMind’s logo and styling for brand consistency across sites.

Limitations and Important Notes

  • Watchlist Management is NOT Supported in the External Chatbot:
    Watchlist creation, management, and alerts are only available in the MarketMind Telegram Bot.
    The external chatbot on partner websites or embedded widgets does not support saving tickers or sending notifications.

  • The chatbot focuses purely on delivering on-demand news and sentiment insights without persistent user data storage or custom alert features.

Best Practices for Website Owners

  • Add the chatbot JavaScript snippet near the end of your webpage before the closing </body> tag for optimal loading performance.
  • Ensure your site visitors know the chatbot provides insights on Indian stock markets specifically.
  • Regularly keep your backend configuration in sync to ensure the chatbot loads the latest features and branding.
  • Monitor chatbot performance and user interactions to identify any issues or improvement areas.

Troubleshooting Tips

If the chatbot does not appear or function as expected:

  • Verify that your website properly loads the chatbot JavaScript without blocking scripts or errors.
  • Confirm there are no multiple chatbot instances embedded on the same page.
  • Clear your browser cache and reload the page to fetch the latest chatbot version.
  • Ensure a stable internet connection to reach MarketMind backend services used by the chatbot.
  • Contact your MarketMind support representative if problems persist.

Summary

The MarketMind External Chatbot is a simple yet powerful way to offer visitors instant financial intelligence on Indian stocks through conversational AI on your website. While it does not offer watchlist or alert features, it provides quick market news and sentiment analysis, enhancing user engagement with timely and relevant data.

For watchlist management and personalized alerts, users should interact with the official MarketMind Telegram Bot.

Thank you for integrating MarketMind AI’s external chatbot — empowering your users with smarter market awareness.
