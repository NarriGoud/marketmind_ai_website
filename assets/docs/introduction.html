What is MarketMind AI?

MarketMind AI is a self-learning financial intelligence platform that continuously analyzes the market to keep you informed. It:

  • Monitors financial news, company filings, and press releases.
  • Automatically detects and tags Indian stock tickers mentioned in news.
  • Assigns sentiment (positive, neutral, negative) with reasoning.
  • Provides interactive chatbot support via Web App, Telegram, and Embedded Widgets.
  • Continuously improves through a feedback-driven learning loop.
  • Presents visual dashboards so users can see insights clearly.

It functions as an AI-powered co-pilot for market research and monitoring.

Watchlist Management Details

One of MarketMind AI’s core features is its watchlist and alert system.

Functionality

  • Add and customize Indian stock tickers to monitor.
  • Receive instant alerts when news or filings mention your selected stocks.
  • Access sentiment ratings along with clear explanations.
  • Configure notification preferences for immediate or scheduled alerts.

Example

Tracking stocks like RELIANCE and INFY:

  • MarketMind AI monitors news for these stocks in real time.
  • When Reliance announces an energy project, users receive:
  • Ticker: RELIANCE
  • Sentiment: Positive
  • Reasoning: Expansion signals growth in long-term fundamentals.
  • If Infosys faces IT spending concerns, the alert might include:
  • Ticker: INFY
  • Sentiment: Negative
  • Reasoning: Industry slowdown may impact revenues.

Alert Delivery

  • Alerts delivered through the dashboard and Telegram Bot.
  • Notification frequency is configurable to user preference.
