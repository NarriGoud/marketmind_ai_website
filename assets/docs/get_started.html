Getting Started with MarketMind AI

Welcome to the MarketMind AI documentation. This guide will help you understand how to access and use MarketMind AI effectively, whether you are interacting through the web chatbot, partner sites, or the Telegram Bot.

What is This Page?

This page is part of the official MarketMind AI user documentation. It provides detailed information on:

  • How to access MarketMind AI across different platforms.
  • How to interact with the AI chatbot using natural language.
  • Platform-specific capabilities and current limitations.
  • Best practices to maximize the value of MarketMind AI’s insights.

By following this guide, you will be able to receive timely financial insights and understand market sentiment for Indian stocks with ease.

Accessing MarketMind AI

MarketMind AI can be accessed through multiple channels designed for varying user needs:

1. Web Chatbot on This Site

  • The web chatbot is accessible via the floating chatbot button located at the bottom-right corner of this website.
  • Click to open the chat window and ask questions using everyday language about news or sentiment for Indian stocks.
  • Important: The web chatbot (on this site or any external partner websites) does not support watchlist management or alerts. It primarily offers on-demand news and sentiment queries.

2. Partner Websites

  • Select partner websites have integrated MarketMind AI’s chatbot interface.
  • The experience and functionality are similar to the main web chatbot, with the same limitation: watchlist and alert management are not available.
  • Users can still query stock news, sentiment, and general information.

3. Telegram Bot

  • The Telegram Bot offers the most comprehensive set of features.
  • Search for the official MarketMind AI Telegram Bot or use a direct invite link to start.
  • Full functionality is supported, including:
  • Adding and removing stocks from personalized watchlists.
  • Receiving real-time alerts on relevant news and sentiment changes.
  • Getting detailed sentiment reasoning and market context.

Using the Chatbot

MarketMind AI is designed to interact naturally—no technical or coding skills required.

Example Queries

  • "Show me news on RELIANCE."
  • "What is the sentiment for TCS today?"
  • "Add RELIANCE to my watchlist." (Telegram Bot only)
  • "Remove TCS from my watchlist." (Telegram Bot only)
  • "What are the latest filings for INFY?"

The chatbot replies clearly with:
- Relevant news headlines and summaries.
- Sentiment classification (positive, neutral, or negative).
- Brief explanations for the sentiment assessment, helping you understand the context.

Best Practices

  • Use clear and specific queries including stock ticker symbols for the best accuracy.
  • Prefer the Telegram Bot if you want to create and manage a personalized watchlist.
  • Regularly interact with MarketMind AI to stay updated on fast-moving market developments.
  • Use consistent, simple commands to add or remove stocks from your watchlist on Telegram.
  • Provide feedback through chatbot interactions to help improve AI accuracy and relevance.
