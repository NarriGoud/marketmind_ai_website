Telegram Trading Assistant Bot

Welcome to your personal trading assistant bot! This guide will help you understand the features, menu buttons, and how to interact with the bot effectively.

What I Can Do

  • Trading Concepts: Get explanations for trading terms, technical indicators, and financial concepts.
  • Financial News: Ask for real-time news headlines, SEC filings, or earnings call summaries.
  • Watchlist Management: Easily add, remove, or view your favorite stocks.
  • Daily Alerts: Receive updates and sentiment analysis for stocks in your watchlist.

How to Use the Bot

You can interact with the bot using the menu buttons or by typing questions directly into the chat.

Asking Questions

Simply type your question into the chat. Examples:

  • "What is a moving average?"
  • "Latest news for Apple?"
  • "Explain the difference between bull and bear markets."

Watchlist Menu Buttons

Menu Button How to Use Description
`Add to Watchlist` Click the menu button, then type a comma-separated list of stock tickers in the chat (e.g., `TCS, RELIANCE, ITC`). Adds the tickers to your personal watchlist.
`Remove from Watchlist` Click the menu button, then type the tickers you wish to remove, or `all` to clear your watchlist. Removes tickers from your watchlist.
`List Watchlist` Click the menu button. Displays all tickers currently in your watchlist.

Other Menu Buttons

Menu Button Description
`Start New Session` Starts a fresh chat session to clear previous conversation context.
`Give Feedback` Provides instructions to give feedback on bot responses or general comments.
`Help` Shows this guide again.

Important Notes

  • Disclaimer: Responses are generated by AI. Always verify information before making trading decisions.
  • Keyboard: The main menu keyboard is always available at the bottom of the chat. If it disappears, sending a message or clicking a menu button will bring it back.
  • Examples: Use commas to separate multiple tickers when adding or removing stocks.
