Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – MarketMind AI

Welcome to the FAQ page. This page provides answers to common questions about MarketMind AI, its features, and usage.

1. Access & Platforms

Q1: How do I access MarketMind AI?
A: You can access MarketMind AI via the Web Chatbot on the main website, partner websites that have integrated the chatbot, or the Telegram Bot.

Q2: Do I need an account to use MarketMind AI?
A: No account is required for the web chatbot or partner websites. For Telegram Bot, only a Telegram account is needed.

Q3: Can I use MarketMind AI on mobile devices?
A: Yes. Both the web chatbot and Telegram Bot are mobile-friendly.

Q4: Do partner websites provide the same features as the main site?
A: Partner websites provide news and sentiment analysis, but watchlist management and alerts are Telegram-only.

Q5: Can I access MarketMind AI from multiple devices?
A: Yes, you can use the web chatbot or Telegram Bot on multiple devices simultaneously.

Q6: Is MarketMind AI available internationally?
A: Currently, MarketMind AI supports Indian tickers only, but the web and Telegram Bot are accessible globally.

2. Features & Usage

Q7: What can I ask the chatbot?
A: You can ask about news, stock sentiment, and watchlist actions (Telegram-only).

Q8: How does MarketMind AI determine sentiment?
A: It analyzes news headlines and financial updates for a stock, classifies them as positive, neutral, or negative, and provides reasoning.

Q9: Can I track global stocks?
A: Not yet. MarketMind AI currently supports Indian tickers only.

Q10: Can I ask multiple stock queries in one message?
A: Yes, you can ask about multiple tickers, e.g., "Show me news for RELIANCE and TCS."

Q11: How quickly does MarketMind AI update news and sentiment?
A: Updates are near real-time. Alerts on Telegram are triggered immediately for significant updates.

Q12: Does MarketMind AI provide stock prices or charts?
A: No. It provides news, sentiment, and watchlist alerts. Prices and charts are not included.

Q13: Can I ask MarketMind AI for financial advice?
A: No. MarketMind AI provides insights but not personalized financial advice.

Q14: Can MarketMind AI analyze historical data?
A: Currently, MarketMind AI focuses on real-time news and sentiment. Historical data analysis is planned for future updates.

Q15: How do I phrase questions for the best results?
A: Be clear and include ticker symbols. For example, "What is the sentiment for RELIANCE today?" Avoid vague or ambiguous queries.

3. Watchlist & Alerts (Telegram Only)

Q16: How do I add a stock to my watchlist?
A: On Telegram, type: "Add [TICKER] to my watchlist", e.g., "Add RELIANCE to my watchlist."

Q17: How do I remove a stock from my watchlist?
A: On Telegram, type: "Remove [TICKER] from my watchlist."

Q18: Can I track multiple stocks in one watchlist?
A: Yes, you can add multiple tickers to your Telegram watchlist.

Q19: Can I get alerts on the web chatbot?
A: No, alerts are available only via Telegram Bot.

Q20: How are alerts delivered?
A: Alerts are sent directly in Telegram with the ticker name, sentiment, and reasoning.

Q21: Can I customize alert frequency?
A: Currently, alerts are sent automatically when significant news or sentiment changes occur.

Q22: Are watchlists private?
A: Yes, watchlists are private and linked only to your Telegram account.

4. Limitations

Q23: Why can’t I use watchlist features on the web chatbot?
A: Watchlist management is exclusive to the Telegram Bot.

Q24: Why does MarketMind AI only support Indian tickers?
A: The platform is focused on the Indian market. Global tickers will be supported in future updates.

Q25: Are there limitations to chatbot understanding?
A: The AI may occasionally misinterpret complex or ambiguous queries. Feedback helps improve accuracy.

Q26: Can MarketMind AI guarantee the accuracy of news or sentiment?
A: No. MarketMind AI provides insights, not guaranteed predictions. Always verify critical information independently.

5. Account & Privacy

Q27: Is my data shared with third parties?
A: No personal data is shared. Only anonymized usage data may be used for improvement.

Q28: How is my privacy protected?
A: MarketMind AI follows strict privacy standards and stores only minimal necessary information for alerts and chatbot responses.

Q29: Do I need to register or provide personal details?
A: No registration or personal details are required to use the service.

6. Troubleshooting & Miscellaneous

Q30: What should I do if the chatbot doesn’t respond?
A: Ensure you have an internet connection. Refresh the page for web chatbot issues or reconnect on Telegram.

Q31: Can I report bugs or give feedback?
A: Yes, via Telegram Bot or the website contact page.

Q32: Can I suggest new features?
A: Yes, feedback is welcome and helps guide future updates.

Q33: How often is MarketMind AI updated?
A: The system continuously updates news and sentiment for Indian tickers in near real-time.

Q34: Can I use MarketMind AI during weekends or holidays?
A: Yes, the chatbot is accessible anytime, but financial news may be limited outside trading days.

Q35: Can I switch between web chatbot and Telegram Bot seamlessly?
A: Yes, but watchlist features are only accessible via Telegram.

7. Future Features & Roadmap

Q36: Will MarketMind AI support global tickers?
A: Yes, this feature is planned for future releases.

Q37: Will advanced analytics or charts be added?
A: Future updates may include advanced analytics, forecasts, and dashboards.

Q38: Can I integrate MarketMind AI into my website?
A: Yes, partnership integration is possible. Contact MarketMind AI for details.

Q39: Will there be mobile app versions?
A: Currently, web and Telegram interfaces are supported. Dedicated apps may be developed in the future.

Q40: How can I stay updated with new features?
A: Follow the official website, Telegram Bot announcements, or partner communications.
